My Story

Hi! I’m a UX/UI Designer with a background in Graphic and Editorial Design. The essential part of my job was working in a team to identify problems, gather requirements, and create aesthetically appealing solutions. Over time, my increasing passion for technologies and innovation has naturally led me to UX/UI Design, where I integrate my empathetic nature, aesthetic eye, and innovative thinking to build satisfying user experiences. I offer skills in user research, information architecture, and visual design, as well as a passion for organization and efficiency. I’m curious, open-minded, and love working in a team to collaborate on bringing exciting projects to life.
